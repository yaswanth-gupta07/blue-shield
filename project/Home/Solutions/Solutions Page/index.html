Solutions

Problem: Ozone depletion in the stratosphere due to CFCs

Solution: Enforce regulations like the Montreal Protocol to phase out ozone-depleting substances.

Problem: Ground-level (tropospheric) ozone formation from pollutants.

Solution: Promote clean energy and enforce stricter emission controls for vehicles and industries.

Problem: UV radiation harming marine ecosystems, particularly plankton.

Solution: Reduce the use of ozone-depleting chemicals to protect marine life.

Problem: Ground-level ozone weakening plant growth and reducing crop yields.

Solution: Implement stricter air quality regulations and encourage sustainable farming practices.

Problem: Ozone depletion disrupting weather patterns and the water cycle.

Solution: Reforestation and the protection of ecosystems to help stabilize local climates.

Problem: Loss of biodiversity due to habitat degradation from increased ozone and UV radiation.

Solution: Expand conservation efforts and protect endangered species through habitat restoration.
